Our Journey

Milestones That Shaped Us

1998
Founded in Tamil Nadu
TMG TMG SRI BAHAVAN GROUPS began as a small soap and oil workshop in Arasarkulam, driven by a vision to bring affordable, natural cleaning products to every household.
2005
Expanded Product Line
Launched industrial-grade bleaching powder and floor cleaning range, entering the B2B market with competitive wholesale pricing.
2012
Regional Distribution Network
Established dealer partnerships across Tamil Nadu and Karnataka, scaling production to meet growing regional demand.
2019
CLEANZOD Brand Launch
Rebranded under the CLEANZOD identity, unifying all product lines with modern packaging and quality certifications.
2024
10,000+ Customers Milestone
Crossed 10,000 satisfied customers and launched the LeanZo platform, enabling direct-to-consumer sales across India.
